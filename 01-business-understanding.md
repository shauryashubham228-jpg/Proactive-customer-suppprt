# Business Understanding

## Core Objective

Move from reactive support to proactive operations.

Reactive:
Customer contacts support after issue.

Proactive:
System detects issue before customer escalates.

---

## Business Goals

| Goal | KPI |
|---|---|
| Reduce WISMO tickets | Ticket reduction |
| Improve CSAT | Customer satisfaction |
| Reduce escalations | Escalation rate |
| Improve delivery experience | SLA compliance |
